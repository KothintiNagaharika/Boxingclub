Boxing

The only method of attack is punching with a clenched fist and you may not strike below the belt, in the kidneys or the back of your opponents head or neck. You cannot use the ropes for leverage. You cannot hit an opponent when they are down. A boxer hit with a low blow can take five minutes to recover.

12 Rules of Boxing

1.You Cannot Hit Below the Belt. 2.You Cannot Hit the Neck,Back and Kidney Areas and Back of the Head. 3.You Cannot Hit With Lose Gloves. 4.You Cannot Hit a Knocked-Down Opponent. 5.You Cannot Throw a Punch While Holding the Rope. 6.You Cannot Hit On Break.

7.You Cannot Hit On Break. 8.You Cannot Headbutt. 9.You Cannot Hit Your Opponent While Holding Them. 10.You Have 10 Seconds as a Knocked-Down Player To Get Up. 11.You Have 20 Seconds To Get Back To the Ring. 12.There are instances where a player may be hit by accident below the belt.
